Thank You!

Thank you doesn't even begin to cover our collective gratitude for our instructors throughout our journey at NSS. It's hard to believe that we started this program back in July and we already find ourselves about to graduate and with significantly more knowledge then when we started. We have all ridden the roller coaster of emotion during this program, each one of us has felt the highs and lows of learning this new craft. But we wouldn't have been able to get through any of it without the guidance of our educators.

Thank you to our amazing instructors; we couldn't have done it without you! Steve, Val, Devin, Caroline, Tiana, and Laura Beth.

Thank you also to our wonderful career development specialist - Ashley and Michael! We appreciate all of the events you put together for us to help further our budding careers. And a big thank you for helping us refine our resumes and cover letters so we can put our best foot forward!

We are grateful for John Wark and his vision for Nashville Software School. We wouldn't be here without you!

Finally, a huge thank you to each team member of Cohort 58! Thank you for supporting each other, for celebrating each other, and for being there for each other throughout this program!
